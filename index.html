LDU Factorization

It is a method of decomposing a square matrix of any arbitrary dimension into the product of three matrices.

  • L : Lower Triangular Matrix
  • D : Diagonal Matrix
  • U : Upper Triangular Matrix


    • Fixed 3x3 Matrix:
